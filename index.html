Kigali,Rwanda

Rwanda, often called the “Land of a Thousand Hills”, is a beautiful country in East-Central Africa, known for its rolling green landscapes, vibrant culture, and remarkable progress in development. Its capital city, Kigali, is one of the cleanest and fastest-growing cities in Africa. Rwanda is also famous for its mountain gorillas in Volcanoes National Park, a strong focus on sustainability, and its inspiring story of resilience and unity after the 1994 genocide.
